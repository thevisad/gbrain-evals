Spent most of today prepping for the Canopy Analytics board meeting next week. Not alarming yet, but worth discussing before they come back for the Series B bridge conversation.

I pulled together a deck comparing their unit economics to three other portfolio companies at similar stage. The numbers tell a clearer story than my gut feeling—they're actually tracking ahead on revenue per employee, which gives me more confidence in the expansion.

Called [Nadia Okonkwo](people/nadia-okonkwo) to get her read on their technical roadmap. She reminded me that founder confidence and actual execution timelines rarely align, especially in climate hardware integration. Fair point. I'll push on the pilot conversion rates during the meeting.

Need to remember: board prep isn't just about finding problems. It's about arriving with enough context to be genuinely useful. Nobody needs another investor who only asks hard questions without offering paths forward.
