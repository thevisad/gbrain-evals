Spent most of today preparing materials for the [Cirrus Systems](people/cirrus-systems) board meeting next week. This will be their first formal board since the Series A closed, and I want to set the right tone for governance without overwhelming a team that's still only eighteen people.

[Derek Huang](people/derek-huang) sent over the draft deck—financials look clean, but the narrative around customer concentration needs work. Three enterprise contracts account for 67% of ARR right now. That's not unusual at this stage, but the board should understand the pipeline diversity strategy, not just see a pie chart.

I'm also thinking about how to frame the infrastructure cost discussion. Their GPU spend is climbing faster than revenue, which is expected for training-intensive workloads, but we need guardrails before it becomes a pattern no one questions. Planning to suggest quarterly unit economics reviews rather than waiting for annual budget cycles.

Good boards ask hard questions early. That's the culture I want to build here.
