# Portfolio Review: Vespera Dynamics Q1 Performance

[Amara Okafor](user/amara-okafor) and [Elena Rossi](people/elena-rossi) met to discuss [Vespera Dynamics](companies/vespera-dynamics)' Q1 2026 results and upcoming strategic priorities.

## Q1 Performance Summary

[Elena](people/elena-rossi) walked through the numbers: ARR grew 34% YoY to $18.2M, with net revenue retention at 112%. Gross margins improved slightly to 71%, up from 68% in Q4. The team closed 14 new enterprise accounts, including two Fortune 500 logos in the manufacturing vertical.

[Amara](user/amara-okafor) noted this puts them ahead of the board plan by roughly 8%, which is encouraging given the broader macro headwinds affecting B2B software.

## Burn and Runway

Current monthly burn is approximately $1.4M. With $31M in the bank, runway extends through Q3 2027. [Elena](people/elena-rossi) mentioned they're evaluating whether to accelerate hiring in sales given the strong pipeline conversion rates.

[Amara](user/amara-okafor) recommended waiting until Q2 results confirm the trend before committing to expanded headcount. She offered to connect [Elena](people/elena-rossi) with [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) from [Helios](companies/helios), who navigated a similar scaling decision last year.

## Product Roadmap

The AI-assisted analytics module is on track for beta release in June. Early customer feedback from design partners has been positive. [Elena](people/elena-rossi) flagged that [Priya Patel](people/priya-patel) has been instrumental in shaping the technical architecture.
