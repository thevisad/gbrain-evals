# Portfolio Review: Capacitor Labs Q1 Performance

[Amara Okafor](user/amara-okafor) and [Hannah Liu](people/hannah-liu) connected to review [Capacitor Labs](companies/capacitor-labs) Q1 2026 performance and discuss the upcoming Series B process.

## Key Discussion Points

[Hannah](people/hannah-liu) walked through the quarterly metrics. Revenue hit $2.1M ARR, up 34% from Q4. Net retention came in at 118%, which [Amara](user/amara-okafor) noted was solid but slightly below the 125% target they'd set at board level. [Hannah](people/hannah-liu) explained the delta was driven by two mid-market customers churning in February—both were acquisitions where the buyer already had competing solutions in place.

Pipeline looks strong heading into Q2. [Hannah](people/hannah-liu) mentioned they closed [Meridian Health](companies/meridian-health) last month, their first enterprise healthcare customer, which validates the compliance work they prioritized in H2 2025.

Discussion shifted to Series B timing. [Amara](user/amara-okafor) advised waiting until they have two full quarters of enterprise traction before going out. [Hannah](people/hannah-liu) agreed but flagged that runway is ~14 months and she'd prefer to start conversations in Q3 to close by year-end.

[Amara](user/amara-okafor) offered to make introductions to [Sarah Chen](people/sarah-chen) at Northgate Partners and [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) at Foundry Group as potential leads for the round. Both have been actively deploying in enterprise infrastructure.

## Decisions Made

- Series B process to kick off in late Q3 2026
- [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital) will maintain pro-rata and potentially increase allocation
- Hold off on CFO hire until after Series B closes
