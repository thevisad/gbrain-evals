# Catch-up with Ravi Gupta - Meridian Robotics Update

[Amara Okafor](user/amara-okafor) connected with [Ravi Gupta](people/ravi-gupta) for a portfolio check-in on [Meridian Robotics](companies/meridian-robotics).

## Company Update

[Ravi](people/ravi-gupta) shared that [Meridian](companies/meridian-robotics) is tracking ahead of plan for Q2. The warehouse automation pilot with Target is going well—they're seeing 40% efficiency gains in the initial deployment. Team has grown to 47 people, mostly on the engineering side.

[Ravi](people/ravi-gupta) mentioned they're starting to think about their Series B timing. Current runway extends through Q1 2027, but given the momentum, he wants to be opportunistic. [Sarah Chen](people/sarah-chen) at Redpoint has been sniffing around, apparently reached out after the TechCrunch piece last month.

## Ownership Discussion

[Amara](user/amara-okafor) confirmed [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital) currently holds **8.5% ownership** in [Meridian](companies/meridian-robotics) post-Series A. [Ravi](people/ravi-gupta) noted that they're modeling the B at a $180-220M pre, which would likely dilute existing investors to the 6-7% range depending on round size.

[Amara](user/amara-okafor) expressed interest in maintaining pro-rata, possibly taking additional allocation if available. [Ravi](people/ravi-gupta) said he'd keep her in the loop but no formal process yet.

## Other Topics

Brief discussion about [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) potentially joining as an advisor—[Ravi](people/ravi-gupta) met him at a dinner [Amara](user/amara-okafor) hosted last quarter. [Marcus](people/marcus-reid) has deep logistics experience that could help with enterprise sales motion.

[Ravi](people/ravi-gupta) also mentioned [Priya Patel](people/priya-patel) has been helpful with some finance org design questions.
