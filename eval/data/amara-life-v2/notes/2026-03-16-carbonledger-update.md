Caught up with [Felix Hartmann](people/felix-hartmann) this morning on the [CarbonLedger](people/carbonledger) Q1 numbers. They've hit 140 enterprise accounts, which is ahead of the projection we underwrote at Series A. The real surprise is retention — net revenue retention is sitting at 118%, driven almost entirely by expansion within existing customers rather than new logos. That tells me the product is genuinely sticky, not just cheap to acquire.

[Priya Sundaram](people/priya-sundaram) flagged that the MRR-to-ARR conversion discrepancy we noticed last quarter has been cleaned up on the accounting side — it was a reporting artifact, not a churn signal. Good to have that resolved before the Series B data room opens.

My main concern heading into the raise is market framing. "Carbon accounting" still reads as compliance infrastructure to most generalist investors. We need to help the team tell a sharper story around the AI-driven forecasting layer — that's where the real defensibility lives. Following up with Felix on positioning this week.
