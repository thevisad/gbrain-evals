Spent most of today working through the first draft of the investment memo for Solengrid. The numbers are cleaner than I expected at this stage — customer acquisition costs are trending down quarter over quarter, and their inference efficiency gains are genuinely impressive relative to comparable grid-edge AI plays we've seen.

Had a long call with [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) this afternoon to pressure-test the demand forecasting model. He pushed back hard on the utility partnership assumptions, which I think was exactly right — I'd been too generous with the adoption timeline in the Southeast markets. Going to revise that section before Thursday's partner meeting.

My broader conviction here is still strong. The intersection of distributed energy management and on-device inference is where I want Halfway to be planting flags in 2025. Solengrid is early, but the technical moat feels real. The question is whether the team can execute at the pace the market window requires. Still forming a view on that.

More diligence needed on the CTO's background before we move to term sheet.
