Spent the better part of this morning on a call with [Marcus Chen](people/marcus-chen) going through CarbonLedger's Q2 metrics. Honestly more encouraging than I expected — their verified-credit throughput is up nearly 40% quarter-over-quarter, and the data pipeline they rebuilt with [Priya Nambiar](people/priya-nambiar) leading engineering has meaningfully cut reconciliation latency. That was the chronic pain point last year, so seeing it actually resolved rather than patched feels significant.

My remaining concern is enterprise sales velocity. The product is solid now, but the pipeline still skews heavily toward mid-market buyers. We need to see at least one anchor enterprise close before the Series B narrative really holds together. Marcus knows this — we talked about it plainly — and he's credible on the path forward.

Overall I'm cautiously optimistic. CarbonLedger sits at exactly the intersection we care about: durable climate infrastructure with real data and AI leverage underneath it. Worth continued conviction here.
