Spent the morning revisiting our notes from the [NovaMind](people/novamind) diligence call last week. Still processing. [Darius Walsh](people/darius-walsh) made a compelling case for their inference optimization layer — the efficiency gains on sparse workloads are real, and I've seen enough datacenter energy bills to know that matters. But I keep coming back to the go-to-market. They're essentially betting that hyperscalers will license rather than build, and that assumption feels underexamined.

Flagged this for [Priya Subramaniam](people/priya-subramaniam) to pressure-test on the technical side — specifically whether the moat holds if Azure or GCP decides to move. We should also get a clearer read on their current contracted ARR versus pipeline. The climate angle is genuinely differentiated, but differentiation only compounds if the business model is durable. Scheduling a follow-up for early December before we decide whether to proceed to term sheet.
