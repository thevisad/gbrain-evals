Worth noting: [Magnus Thorvald](people/magnus-thorvald) is still CEO at Verdant Grid, which matters for how seriously I weight their pipeline numbers in this report. Leadership continuity there has been a quiet differentiator. Hoping that holds.

We should be stress-testing our thesis on both ends of that distribution before LP calls in January.
