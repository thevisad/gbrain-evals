Caught up with [Chloe Ng](people/chloe-ng) this afternoon for a CarbonLedger check-in. They've made real progress since our last diligence round — the MRV pipeline is meaningfully tighter, and they've cut verification latency from roughly eleven days down to under three using their new inference layer. That's the kind of concrete improvement I want to see before we talk about leading the Series B.

Still, a few things are sitting with me. Customer concentration is higher than I'd like — two enterprise accounts represent nearly 60% of ARR. Chloe acknowledged it directly, which I respect, and she walked me through the pipeline she's building to address it. Promising, but not yet de-risked.

The AI infra angle here is genuinely differentiated. Most carbon accounting tools bolt on ML as an afterthought; CarbonLedger is building it into the data model from the ground up. That's hard to replicate quickly. Going to flag this for our Monday IC call and see where the rest of the team lands.
