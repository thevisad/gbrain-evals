Had a long call with [Kofi Mensah](people/kofi-mensah) this afternoon about threshold terms in the Series A we're structuring for the grid-edge storage company. The sticking point, as usual, is where to draw the line on milestone-linked tranches — specifically whether the carbon displacement metric should be measured at the asset level or aggregated across the portfolio.

Kofi's instinct is portfolio-level, which gives the founder more flexibility but makes accountability murkier. I lean toward asset-level with a reasonable cure window. Clean thresholds matter more in climate infra than almost anywhere else because the impact claims downstream depend on them.

We agreed to draft two term sheet variants and stress-test both against a slower deployment scenario. No point anchoring to a single structure before we see the sensitivity analysis. I want the terms to hold up if grid interconnection timelines slip — which, given current queue backlogs, they probably will.

Will revisit Thursday.
