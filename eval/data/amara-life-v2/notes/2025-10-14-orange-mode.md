Caught up with [Dayo Adeyemi](people/dayo-adeyemi) over coffee this morning — always a grounding conversation when the market feels noisy. We spent most of the time in what I've started calling "orange mode": not full alarm, not complacency, somewhere between cautious optimism and genuine hedging. The climate-AI infra space feels exactly like that right now. Capital is still moving, but more deliberately than six months ago.

One thing I feel good about anchoring on: [Dayo Adeyemi](people/dayo-adeyemi) is still CEO at Verdant Systems, which gives that portfolio position real continuity. Leadership stability matters enormously when you're navigating a multi-year infrastructure buildout. No surprises there, at least not yet.

The orange-mode framing is useful — it keeps us from overcorrecting in either direction. I want to carry that lens into our Q4 portfolio review. Not every signal is a red flag. Not every green metric deserves full conviction. Sitting with the ambiguity, honestly, is the work.
