Caught up with [Tomoko Sato](people/tomoko-sato) this afternoon for a CarbonLedger progress check. Honestly more encouraged than I expected to be at this stage. Their MRV pipeline is tightening up — the on-chain attestation layer they demoed last week has real legs, and the latency improvements since Q3 are significant. Tomoko flagged that enterprise pilot conversations with two large logistics players are moving faster than projected, which is the kind of pull-through validation we were hoping to see before they raise the A.

My remaining concern is still on the regulatory side — Article 6 implementation remains a mess, and CarbonLedger's unit economics depend on voluntary markets not cratering further while compliance markets sort themselves out. We talked through a few contingency scenarios. Nothing alarming, but worth keeping a close eye on through H1. I'll loop in the rest of the investment team next week and draft a portfolio update memo. Strong signal overall.
