Quiet Sunday before the holiday week swallows everything. I've been thinking about the leadership transitions we navigated this year across the portfolio — a few were rocky, most were quietly transformative. One anchor of consistency throughout has been [Priya Anand](people/priya-anand), who is still CEO at Solstice Grid and, frankly, the reason that company didn't wobble when the permitting environment got brutal in Q3. There's something clarifying about watching a founder hold the line on long-term thinking when every macro signal is screaming short-term.

On the AI infra side, I keep returning to the same question: who is actually building for the 2030 grid, not just the 2026 fundraise? Fewer teams than I'd like. Going into the new year I want to be more deliberate about where I spend partner time versus just deal time. They're not the same thing, and I've been letting the distinction blur.

More coffee. Then the LP letters.
