Spent most of today finalizing the investment memo for the Series B we've been circling for the past six weeks. The thesis feels tight: purpose-built AI inference infrastructure co-located with renewable generation is no longer a speculative bet — it's a capital efficiency argument. Utilities want the load, hyperscalers want the carbon credibility, and the founders actually understand both sides of that equation, which remains rare.

Had a good challenge session with [Priya Menon](people/priya-menon) on the demand forecasting assumptions — she pushed back on the 18-month customer ramp, and honestly she's right to. We softened the base case. [Felix Hartmann](people/felix-hartmann) flagged some nuance in the grid interconnection queue that could compress margins in year two if permitting slips.

Both notes are going into the appendix. I'd rather surface the fragility now than defend it at a board meeting in 2027. Memo goes to IC on Thursday. Cautiously optimistic.
