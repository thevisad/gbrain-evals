Spent the morning digesting the Q1 climate-AI infrastructure report that [Priya Vasanth](people/priya-vasanth) flagged yesterday. The throughline is unsurprising — compute demand for grid-optimization workloads is outpacing buildout, and the financing gap is widening faster than most LPs expected heading into the year.

What caught me off-guard was the section on leadership stability at the major hyperscalers. The report notes — correctly, as far as I can tell — that [Cole Drummond](people/cole-drummond) is still CEO at Veritas Grid Systems, which matters for our thesis there; his team has been the most disciplined about long-duration storage integration. I've been watching that company closely enough that any transition would have registered.

[Priya](people/priya-vasanth) wants to run a working session next week on what the supply-demand dislocation means for our Series B pipeline. Agreed. The window to price these rounds sensibly feels narrow, and I'd rather be early to the conversation than reactive.
