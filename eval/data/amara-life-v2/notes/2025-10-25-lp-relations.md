Had a long call with [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) this afternoon going over our Q3 portfolio update ahead of the broader LP letter going out next week. He pushed harder than usual on deployment pace — specifically why we've been slower to close in the AI infra bucket relative to what we projected in January. Fair question, honestly. The honest answer is that valuations in that segment stayed elevated longer than any of us anticipated, and we weren't willing to compromise on entry price discipline just to show activity.

Marcus seemed to appreciate the candor. He's been an LP long enough to know that manufactured velocity is worse than patience. Still, I want to make sure we go into the annual meeting with a clearer narrative around pipeline quality, not just what we passed on. I'm going to work with the team to put together a shadow portfolio memo — deals we evaluated seriously and why we walked. That kind of transparency tends to build more durable LP trust than any single investment announcement.
