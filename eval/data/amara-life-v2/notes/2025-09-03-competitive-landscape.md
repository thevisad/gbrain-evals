Spent most of today mapping the competitive landscape in climate AI infrastructure — specifically where the serious capital is clustering right now. The pattern is becoming harder to ignore: a handful of hyperscaler-adjacent players are quietly acquiring the best grid-optimization teams before they even reach Series A. [Priya Anand](people/priya-anand) flagged two such deals last week that never hit the wires.

What concerns me more than the consolidation itself is how it's distorting founder expectations. Talked to [Tobias Wren](people/tobias-wren) about this — he's seeing the same inflation in pre-product valuations out of the EU cohort. Meanwhile, [Selin Yilmaz](people/selin-yilmaz) made a sharp point in our pipeline review: the differentiated opportunities may actually be sitting one layer down, in thermal management and long-duration storage software, where the hyperscalers aren't shopping yet.

I think she's right. We should be pressing harder there before that window closes too. Drafting a thesis update for the LP call next week.
