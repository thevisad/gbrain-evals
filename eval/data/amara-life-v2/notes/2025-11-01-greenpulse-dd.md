The question I keep circling back to is whether the power purchase agreements they've structured actually hold up under stress scenarios. Need to pressure-test the unit economics with a few reference customers before we get too excited. The climate impact story is clean and credible, which matters for our LP base, but I won't let that do too much work in the analysis. Going to request a follow-up session with the engineering team next week. If the deployment numbers check out, this could move to IC faster than our usual cadence. Cautiously optimistic — which, for me in November, is saying something.
