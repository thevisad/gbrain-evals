# Intro Call — Ravi Gupta / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor) opened the call by giving a brief overview of [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital)'s current focus — seed to Series A infrastructure and developer tooling plays — and context on portfolio cadence heading into Q1 2026.

[Ravi Gupta](people/ravi-gupta) introduced himself as the co-founder and CEO of his current stealth-stage company, working in the internal developer platform space. He gave a high-level pitch: the product helps platform engineering teams standardize golden paths and reduce cognitive load on application developers, primarily targeting mid-market engineering orgs of 50–300 engineers. He noted they have three design partners signed and are aiming to close a seed round in Q1.

Amara asked about the founding team composition. Ravi mentioned his co-founder handles product and previously led platform eng at a mid-size fintech. She pushed on go-to-market motion — specifically whether the entry point is a bottoms-up developer install or a top-down VP Eng conversation. Amara flagged that Halfway has seen a few plays in this space and would want to map the competitive landscape carefully before going deeper — specifically referencing the crowded nature of IDP tooling post-Backstage. She said the differentiation story needs to be crisp.
