# Intro Call — Dev Shankar / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor), Partner at [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital), connected with [Dev Shankar](people/dev-shankar) for an initial exploratory conversation. Dev is building in the enterprise data infrastructure space and was referred through the broader network.

## Key Discussion Points

**Company Overview**
Dev walked through the current state of his company — a middleware layer that helps data teams standardize access controls and lineage tracking across multi-cloud environments. They are roughly 14 months in, with a small team of six, and have three design partners paying modest but meaningful contracts.

**Traction & Pipeline**
Revenue is pre-scale but directionally positive. Dev mentioned two larger enterprise pilots in late-stage conversations, both in financial services. Amara probed on sales cycle length and noted it seemed longer than typical for the stage, which Dev acknowledged. He attributed it to compliance review requirements on the buyer side.

**Fundraise**
Dev is beginning to socialize a seed round — targeting around $4M. He has had preliminary conversations with one other fund but nothing term-sheeted. He was transparent that he wanted to move deliberately rather than quickly.

**Fit with Halfway Capital**
Amara shared that [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital) has active portfolio exposure in adjacent data tooling but not directly in access governance. She flagged that could be a positive angle — complementary rather than conflicting. She also mentioned she'd want to loop in a technical advisor before going deeper.

## Decisions

- Amara will do a first-pass on competitive landscape before next conversation
- Both agreed a follow-up with a product demo makes sense as a next step
