# Intro Call — Jordan Park / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor) (Partner, [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital)) connected with [Jordan Park](people/jordan-park) for an initial exploratory conversation. Jordan is currently a Principal at [Meridian Ventures](companies/meridian-ventures) and has been quietly exploring a transition into an operating role or a move to an early-stage fund with a smaller, more focused portfolio thesis.

**Key discussion points:**

- Jordan walked through his investment track record at Meridian, highlighting two portfolio exits in the logistics-tech and climate infrastructure spaces over the past three years. Amara noted these aligned reasonably well with Halfway's current Fund III thesis.
- Amara gave a brief overview of Halfway Capital's structure — a $180M fund, roughly twelve active portfolio companies, team of six. She emphasized the generalist-but-opinionated approach and the expectation that partners carry board seats from seed through Series B.
- Jordan asked about carry structure and partnership track. Amara was candid that a formal partner track would be a 24–36 month conversation and that any incoming hire would start at a Senior Associate or VP level depending on fit and deal flow contributions.
- There was a sidebar conversation about the current seed-stage market, with both agreeing that valuations have rationalized meaningfully since late 2023 but that founder quality remains uneven.
- Jordan mentioned he has a warm relationship with the founding team at [Fieldstone Labs](companies/fieldstone-labs), an early-stage agri-tech startup that Amara flagged as potentially worth a closer look.

**Decisions / Outcomes:**

- Amara agreed to share the Halfway Capital team deck and a recent LP letter to give Jordan more context before a second conversation.
- Jordan will send over a deal memo he authored at Meridian as a writing and thinking sample.
- Both open to a follow-up call in two to three weeks, potentially including [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) from the Halfway team.
