# Intro Call: Bill Hart / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor) and [Bill Hart](people/bill-hart) connected for the first time following a warm intro from [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid). Bill is the co-founder and CEO of [Veridian Labs](companies/veridian-labs), an early-stage climate data infrastructure company based in Austin.

## Gist

Bill gave a quick overview of Veridian Labs — they're building a real-time emissions monitoring layer that sits between industrial sensors and carbon reporting platforms. He mentioned they recently closed a **$4.2M pre-seed round** led by [Contour Ventures](companies/contour-ventures), with the round finalizing in **late September 2025**. Amara noted she'd heard the name come up through [Leo Vance](people/leo-vance) as well, which Bill seemed pleased about.

Amara asked about the cap table and dilution. Bill said Contour took **18% on that round**, and the team retained meaningful equity going into the seed. He's now doing early conversations ahead of a seed raise he expects to kick off in Q1 2026, targeting somewhere in the $10–14M range.

Amara was candid that Halfway typically comes in at seed but appreciated the early heads-up. She flagged that [Priya Patel](people/priya-patel) on the Halfway team focuses on climate infra and should probably be looped in before anything formal.

## Decisions / Next Steps

- Amara will forward Bill's one-pager to [Priya Patel](people/priya-patel) for a temperature check before any formal process begins.
- Bill will send over updated financials and the deck they used for the pre-seed — Amara wants to see how the story has evolved.
- Both agreed to reconnect in **early January 2025** once Bill has a clearer sense of seed timing and terms.
- No NDA needed at this stage; keeping it informal.

Relationship is warm but early. Amara's take: interesting space, credible founder, worth tracking. Not ready to sponsor internally yet — wants Priya's read first.
