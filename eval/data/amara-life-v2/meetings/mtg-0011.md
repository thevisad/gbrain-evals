# Intro Call — Bill Hart / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor) and [Bill Hart](people/bill-hart) connected for an initial introductory meeting. Bill had been referred through a mutual contact in the fintech space and reached out earlier in the week to get time on the calendar.

**Context & Background**

Bill gave a brief overview of his background — roughly 15 years in enterprise SaaS, most recently as COO at a logistics software company that was acquired in 2023. He's been advising two early-stage startups since then and is actively exploring his next operating role, ideally at the Series A or B stage.

Amara provided a quick overview of [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital)'s portfolio focus — primarily B2B software and infrastructure, writing checks at Seed through Series B, and the value-add the firm brings on the go-to-market and recruiting side post-investment.

**Discussion**

The conversation covered several portfolio companies where a seasoned operator could be relevant. Bill expressed strong interest in the supply chain and logistics vertical, which aligns with a couple of current portfolio companies. Amara noted that one company in particular is actively searching for a COO-type to work alongside the founding team, though she was careful not to name it explicitly pending a check-in with the founders first.

Bill also asked about Halfway's LP base and typical board involvement. Amara confirmed the firm takes a board seat at Series A and stays closely involved through the growth stage.
