# Intro Call — Leo Vance / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor) and [Leo Vance](people/leo-vance) connected for a first-look conversation about [Vance](people/leo-vance)'s current company, [Meridian Labs](companies/meridian-labs), which is building infrastructure tooling for regulated-industry AI deployments (healthcare, finance).

**Key points discussed:**

- [Leo](people/leo-vance) walked through the founding story — spun out of a failed Series A pivot, reconstituted the team about 18 months ago. Currently 11 people, mostly eng.
- They are raising a **$6M seed round** and have roughly $1.8M soft-circled from angels. Looking for a lead who can anchor at $2–2.5M.
- [Amara](people/amara-okafor) flagged that [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital) typically writes $1.5–3M checks at seed and this is squarely in range.
- Revenue: ~$180K ARR, two design partners paying, pipeline of three more in late-stage conversations.
- [Leo](people/leo-vance) mentioned the cap table is clean — only the angel money and a small SAFE from the incubator. **[Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital) would come in at a $22M post-money cap** if they lead.
- Competitive differentiation vs. existing compliance middleware players was discussed; [Leo](people/leo-vance) believes the moat is the audit-trail graph they've built, not the model layer.

**Decisions / next steps:**

- [Amara](people/amara-okafor) will bring [Meridian Labs](companies/meridian-labs) to Monday's partner meeting for a first-pass gut-check before requesting a full data room.
- [Leo](people/leo-vance) to send one-pager and the draft term sheet he's been sharing with other interested parties by EOD Friday.
- If partners are warm, [Amara](people/amara-okafor) will intro [Leo](people/leo-vance) to [Marcus Reid](people/marcus-reid) for a technical diligence call — Marcus has domain context from his time in health-tech.
- Follow-up call tentatively scheduled for the week of May 5.

**Amara's take (noted in summary):** Impressed by founder clarity, slightly cautious on the ARR base being thin for a $22M cap. Wants to see the pipeline convert at least one more before term sheet.
