# Portfolio Check-In: Kofi Mensah / Halfway Capital

[Amara Okafor](people/amara-okafor) opened the call with a quick overview of [Halfway Capital](companies/halfway-capital)'s current focus for Q2 — primarily Series A deals in climate infrastructure and B2B SaaS, with a secondary interest in anything touching supply chain resilience.

[Kofi Mensah](people/kofi-mensah) gave an update on his company's traction since their last conversation in January. ARR has grown from $1.2M to just under $2M, driven largely by two enterprise contracts signed in February. He noted that gross margins have held steady around 68%, which he attributed to recent optimizations in their data pipeline infrastructure. Churn remains low — under 3% annualized — though he flagged that one mid-market customer (roughly $120K ARR) is showing warning signs ahead of their renewal in June.

Amara probed on the go-to-market motion, specifically whether the current sales cycle length (averaging around 90 days for enterprise) is sustainable given runway. Kofi acknowledged this is a pressure point and mentioned they're exploring a PLG motion for smaller accounts to reduce dependency on the direct sales team.

They briefly discussed the competitive landscape. Kofi identified two well-funded incumbents and one emerging competitor that recently raised a seed round. Amara asked whether there's been any inbound acquisition interest — Kofi said nothing serious, one casual conversation with a strategic that went nowhere.
